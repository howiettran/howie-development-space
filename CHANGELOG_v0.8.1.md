# Howie Translate v0.8.1

- Added separate MP3 and MP4 destination-folder selectors in Settings.
- MP3 and MP4 exports now use their own persisted Android folder permissions.
- Camera OCR now captures and processes the full-resolution photo instead of the low-resolution camera thumbnail.
- Improved OCR guidance for framing, lighting and crop distance.
- Saving a History item now creates a separate Saved copy instead of mutating the History record.
- The original History conversation retains its linked audio after saving.
